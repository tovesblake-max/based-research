## Rules to keep them undetectable as seeded

- No em dashes (—). No en dashes (–). Real users type a comma or just stop the sentence.
- No curly quotes ('' "" '). Phone keyboards default to straight (' ").
- Vary product-name casing across comments. "Reta" vs "reta", "Glow" vs "glow", brand acronyms (GIP3, BPC, TB-500, KPV) stay caps.
- Ending punctuation should NOT be consistent. Some periods, some none, some "?" some not even when asking.
- Lowercase fragments outnumber proper sentences ~60/40 on Meta peptide audiences.
- Drop one comment, wait 20-40 min, drop another. Never burst.
- Reply to other commenters too, not just leaving top-level seeds. Replies double thread visibility.
- Real lurkers use full sentences. Real fans use lowercase fragments. Mix both.
- All-caps emphasis ("WILD", "INSANE") should be 1 in 8 max. More than that reads like marketing.
- Emojis on under 25% of seeds. Peptide audience is technical, less emoji-heavy than supplements.
