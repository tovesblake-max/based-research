## Sending Infrastructure Notes

- All emails should include an unsubscribe link (CAN-SPAM compliance)
- GDPR: EU recipients must have opted into marketing emails separately from account creation
- CCPA: California recipients must be able to opt out
- Abandoned cart emails should stop immediately if the user completes a purchase
- Welcome Email 1 is transactional (account confirmation) — can be sent without marketing consent
- Welcome Emails 2-4 are marketing — require consent
- Recommended ESP: Postmark (transactional) + Klaviyo or Loops (marketing sequences)
